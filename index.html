About us

Startsynergy Consulting is a fully registered Nigerian indigenous firm of Management Consultants, providing independent and specialist support in Management & Financial Consultancy, Audit, and TAX, Advisory services to government, SMES, public and private organizations.

Who We ARE

We combine social, real-time and efficient technology to put information at your finger-tip so you can make fast, informed and efficient decisions. We do more than developing tools that transform repetitive processes into single click of a button, we help businesses of various sizes simplify complex business through well-thought business and information technology. We create a trusted relationship with our customers that bring value to them.

We draw on a basket of proven tools and methodologies to support decision-making but we believe that each client situation is unique and deserves original solutions.

Our aim is to help corporate organizations to unleash their potentials and achieve sustainable, strategic & scalable growths through our powerful insights tools and frameworks which guide the problem identification process, and to serve as the basis for recommendations and development of growth strategies.

A critical part of our service include training and talent development, to support organizations to translate potential to high performance by transforming people, empowering and motivating them to achieve great goals
